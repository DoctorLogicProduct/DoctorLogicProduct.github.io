Increase Visibility with Video

It’s no secret. The use of video in marketing plans is on the rise. Video has been proven to attract more consumer attention than any other medium. It can be used as a method to build awareness about your practice, as a content tool to grow your patient base, or to showcase you as an expert in your field.

Establish Trust & Confidence

71% of people agree that videos leave them with a positive impression of a practice, and 58% consider businesses with video content to be more trustworthy.

Gain Competitive Advantage

Most businesses still don’t have any marketing videos representing their brands, while 61% of businesses that do say online video is the top converter for sales.

Video traffic made up 73% of all internet traffic in 2016. Expect it to be 82% by 2021.

- Google

Increase Online Visibility

Videos can attract 2-3x as many monthly visitors, double prospective clients’ time on site, and increase organic traffic from search engines by 157%.

Simplify, Educate & Engage

Videos can simplify a message and help clients retain 50% more information than text, which is especially useful when educating clients about complex legal issues.
